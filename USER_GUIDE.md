## 🚀 Getting Started

### **What is GreenNest?**
GreenNest is an eco-friendly e-commerce marketplace specializing in sustainable bamboo furniture. We connect conscious buyers with quality sellers offering environmentally-friendly home furnishings.

### **Creating Your Account**

#### **Step 1: Sign Up**
1. Click **"Sign Up"** button in the top-right corner
2. Choose your account type:
   - **Buyer** - Shop for bamboo furniture
   - **Seller** - Sell your bamboo products
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Click **"Create Account"**

#### **Step 2: Verify Your Email**
- Check your email inbox for verification link
- Click the link to activate your account

#### **Step 3: Complete Your Profile**
- Add profile picture (optional)
- Add delivery address (buyers)
- Add store information (sellers)

### **2. Shopping Cart**

#### **Add to Cart**

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### **Common Issues & Solutions**

#### **1. Can't Login**
**Problem:** "Invalid email or password"
**Solutions:**
- ✅ Check email spelling
- ✅ Verify password (case-sensitive)
- ✅ Click "Forgot Password" to reset
- ✅ Clear browser cache
- ✅ Try different browser

#### **2. Product Not Adding to Cart**
**Problem:** "Add to Cart" not working
**Solutions:**
- ✅ Check if product is in stock
- ✅ Refresh the page
- ✅ Clear browser cache
- ✅ Login to your account
- ✅ Check internet connection

#### **3. Checkout Fails**
**Problem:** Can't complete order
**Solutions:**
- ✅ Fill all required fields
- ✅ Check delivery address format
- ✅ Verify phone number
- ✅ Ensure items still in stock
- ✅ Try different payment method

#### **4. Order Status Not Updating**
**Problem:** Status stuck on "Pending"
**Solutions:**
- ✅ Wait for seller confirmation (24-48 hours)
- ✅ Message seller directly
- ✅ Refresh orders page
- ✅ Check notifications
- ✅ Contact support if >48 hours

#### **5. Notifications Not Appearing**
**Problem:** No bell icon or notifications
**Solutions:**
- ✅ Ensure you're logged in as buyer
- ✅ Check if seller updated order status
- ✅ Refresh page (Ctrl+Shift+R)
- ✅ Check browser console for errors
- ✅ Clear browser cache

#### **6. Images Not Loading**
**Problem:** Product images broken
**Solutions:**
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Refresh page
- ✅ Clear browser cache
- ✅ Try different browser
- ✅ Disable ad blockers

#### **7. Seller Dashboard Not Loading**
**Problem:** Dashboard shows errors
**Solutions:**
- ✅ Verify seller account status
- ✅ Check internet connection
- ✅ Clear browser cache
- ✅ Logout and login again
- ✅ Contact admin support
